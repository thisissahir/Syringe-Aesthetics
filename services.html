Facial balancing

This is how Manuel thinks about every face. Rather than filling one feature in isolation, he maps how your cheeks, chin, jawline, lips, and nose relate, then places filler and Botox across the face so each change supports the others. The goal is proportion, not volume. Small, strategic adjustments that make the whole face look balanced and rested. Results vary by patient.

Good to know

Treatment FAQ

How long does Botox last?

Most patients see effects begin within 3–14 days, lasting roughly 3–4 months. Individual results vary.

How long does filler last?

Depending on the product and area treated, JUVÉDERM® fillers typically last 6–18 months. Individual results vary.

Is there downtime?

Most patients return to normal activities the same day. Mild swelling or bruising at the injection site is possible and typically resolves within a few days.

Do you offer a consultation first?
