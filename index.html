Frequently asked questions

How much does Botox or filler cost at Syringe Aesthetics?

Pricing is customized to your goals and confirmed at your consultation — every face needs a different plan. Syringe Aesthetics is an Allē Rewards–eligible provider, so BOTOX® Cosmetic and JUVÉDERM® treatments can also earn loyalty points.

Will I look overdone or frozen?

No — that's the point of Manuel's approach. Every plan is built around natural, conservative enhancement, so results look like a rested version of you, not a different person.

Do you speak Spanish?

Yes. Manuel is fluent in English and Spanish and treats a diverse, bilingual community.

What treatments does Syringe Aesthetics offer?

BOTOX® Cosmetic, a full range of JUVÉDERM® fillers (lips, cheeks, chin, temples, jawline, under-eye), SKINVIVE by JUVÉDERM®, and KYBELLA® for under-chin fullness. See the full treatment menu.
