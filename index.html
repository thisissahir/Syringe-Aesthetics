Good to know

Frequently asked questions

What is facial balancing?

Instead of treating one line or one feature on its own, Manuel looks at how your cheeks, chin, jaw, lips, and nose work together. He then plans filler and Botox across the face so everything sits in proportion. Small, strategic changes, one balanced result.

How much does Botox or filler cost at Syringe Aesthetics?

Pricing is set at your consultation, because Botox is dosed by your muscles and filler by your anatomy, not by a flat menu. Manuel walks you through the exact plan and cost before anything is placed. Syringe Aesthetics is an Allē Rewards–eligible provider for BOTOX® Cosmetic and JUVÉDERM®.

Will I look overdone or frozen?

No, that is the whole point of how Manuel works. Every plan is built around subtle, balanced enhancement, so you look like a rested version of yourself, not a different person.

¿Hablas español? Do you speak Spanish?

Sí. Manuel is fluent in English and Spanish and treats a diverse, bilingual community across Dallas. Your consultation can be in either language.

What treatments does Syringe Aesthetics offer?

Facial balancing and dermal filler (cheeks, chin, jawline, lips, under-eye, and non-surgical nose), BOTOX® Cosmetic, SKINVIVE by JUVÉDERM®, Sculptra® for collagen and skin tightness, KYBELLA® for under-chin fullness, and medical weight loss with semaglutide. See the full treatment menu.
